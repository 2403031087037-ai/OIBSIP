# iFood Customer Behavior Dashboard

A Power BI dashboard analyzing customer behavior, demographics, and sales performance for iFood's marketing dataset.

---

## 📊 Overview

This interactive dashboard provides a comprehensive view of customer behavior patterns across 2,000 customers, visualizing key metrics including revenue trends, demographic segmentation, and purchasing activity to support data-driven marketing decisions.

---

## 📌 Key Metrics

| Metric | Value |
|--------|--------|
| Total Customers | 2,000 |
| Total Revenue | 895K |
| Average Income | 49.73K |
| Active Customers | 255 |

---

## 🔍 Features

- **Marital Status Distribution** – Donut chart segmenting customers by relationship status  
- **Education Level by Income** – Bar chart correlating education codes with income brackets  
- **Monthly Sales Trend** – Time-series visualization of revenue fluctuations  
- **Web Visits vs Purchases** – Area chart analyzing digital engagement against conversion  
- **Age Range Filter** – Interactive slicer (24–61) for demographic filtering  
- **Complaint Tracker** – KPI gauge monitoring total customer complaints  

---
